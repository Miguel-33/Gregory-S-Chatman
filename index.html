A message from Gregory S. Chatman

My name is Gregory S Chatman. Who I am is unimportant. What I have to say is extremely important. It is so important that we as human beings completely understand the dispensation of our everlasting soul and depends on what it is that the Bible has to say and how we respond. The Bible is conditional.

Obey = Good
Disobey = Deadly

I encourage every human being to pay close attention.

I was called by God and licensed in 1996 to preach the Word of God. However I did not want to be indoctrinated in to any particular denomination. Just Bible.

My story may be of interest to some because of the call upon my life. You see, when I began in ministry I wanted to be like the famous televangelists of that time that who even proliferate the airwaves today.

However as I learned and discerned the scriptures, God snatched that desire out of me. My call has been narrowed to the whole world. No particular denominations.

Every one who hears the Word of God
I strongly encourage to heed its warnings and receive its blessings.

The enemy has deceived the world. Everyone is chasing the wrong things. Don’t get caught chasing the wrong wind.

After 30 years of ministry, I realize that what matters to God is obedience. All that other religious pomp and circumstance is going to get a whole generation cast away.

Please people heed the coming warnings about the coming of our Lord. He is soon to return in judgement. Don’t be on the wrong side of His wrath.

Modern religious systems lift up everything except God. Don’t be deceived. The enemy has perfected deception. Don’t be fooled.

– Gregory S. Chatman

Gregory’s Journey: From Religion to Sincerity

Greg's journey is not just a path—it’s a transformation, from religious performance to a deeper walk with God.

“He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says.”

Step 1: The Calling

In the early 1990s, Greg received his calling by the late Dr. Mattie Moss Clark in Atlanta, GA. This was just the beginning of a spiritual journey that would shape his life and ministry.

Step 2: Obedience Over Religion

Instead of adhering to religious dogma, Greg embraced the calling to preach God's word directly—eschewing denominational boundaries for a personal, sincere relationship with Christ.

Step 3: Spreading the Message

Greg's ministry grew as he began to speak about true obedience to the Word, warning others about spiritual deception and calling everyone to a true, sincere walk with God.

This is a call to every soul.

God is calling. Not to religion, but to obedience. Not to performance, but to truth. This message is for every person on earth — including you.

“He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says.”
